Company Overview

EthioLeather Manufacturing PLC is a premier producer of high-grade leather goods, specializing in full-grain vegetable-tanned accessories. Since 2018, we have integrated traditional Ethiopian craftsmanship with modern sustainable manufacturing processes.

shield_moon ESG & Sustainability

100% wastewater recycling facility and commitment to fair labor practices. We source 80% of raw hides from local smallholder cooperatives.
